gordGPT{{voice}}

GordGPT is an OpenAI language model based on ChatGPT 3.5 Turbo 16k. The training data includes the Tragically and Gord Downie libraries of song lyrics. Instructions: You can interact with GordGPT by typing a question or any sentence into the box below and tapping or clicking the Ask button. The first response given may take a few moments depending on what Gord is currently up to. You can also record a voice message by hitting the Record button to start recording and again to send it. Type Record a second time to send it. Gord will always reply with a typed response along with a voice recording.
